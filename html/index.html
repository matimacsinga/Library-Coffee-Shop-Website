About us


We are a team of students who recently graduated computer science. We don't really have any expertise in the field of literature, just a burning passion for it shared amongst ourselves!
This being said, one thing we grasped in college is research, so we did a lot of that to make sure that we don't go head-first into something we can't mantain .
Despite our main common interest, our love for books is but a foundation for our main intentions regarding this project.
We gave shape to this idea with the aim of sharing this art with everyone and keeping literature relevant in a time when it could easily fall off .
